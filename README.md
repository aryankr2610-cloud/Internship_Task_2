#Business Intelligence & KPI Analysis - Task 2
📌 Project Overview
This repository contains the end-to-end analysis of a multi-table business dataset. The goal of this project was to move beyond basic data cleaning into advanced Data Modeling, SQL Joins, and Business KPI Dashboarding.

The analysis focuses on identifying profitability trends across different regions, product categories, and customer segments to provide actionable business growth recommendations.

📊 Key Business Metrics
Based on the processed dataset, the following high-level KPIs were established:

Total Revenue: $11.49M

Total Profit: $286.40K

Total Orders Processed: 5,009

Global Profit Margin: 2.49%

Average Discount Rate: 15.6%

📂 Dataset Description
The project utilizes a relational structure involving several tables (merged/joined during the process):

Orders Table: Transactional data including Sales, Quantity, Profit, and Discounts.

Product Metadata: Information regarding Categories (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology) and Sub-Categories.

Customer/Geography Data: Segment-wise and Region-wise (North, South, East, West) classification.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Excel: Advanced Pivot Tables, KPI calculation, and data validation.

SQL: Used for joining tables and aggregating metrics (SUM, AVG, GROUP BY).

Power BI: Built an interactive dashboard to visualize regional and category performance.

Python (Optional/Bonus): Data processing and trend analysis using Pandas.

📈 Analysis & Insights
1. Regional Performance
The West Region is the top performer in both Sales and Profit, while the Central Region shows the lowest profit margins, indicating high operational costs or aggressive discounting.

2. Category Deep-Dive
Technology: The most profitable category with the highest margins (specifically Copiers and Accessories).

Furniture: High sales volume but struggling with profitability; "Tables" and "Bookcases" are currently operating at a net loss.

3. Customer Segments
The Home Office segment represents the smallest volume but yields the highest percentage profit margin, suggesting a high-value growth opportunity.

🚀 Key Recommendations
Margin Protection: Implement stricter discount controls in the Central region to boost the 1.6% margin.

Product Optimization: Re-evaluate the pricing strategy for the Furniture category, specifically focusing on loss-making sub-categories like Tables.

Targeted Marketing: Increase acquisition efforts in the Home Office segment due to its superior ROI.
